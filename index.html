Hi there, my name is Alfio Biondo I design and develop things for the web

I am a young architect from Italy who has fallen in love with the concept of 'experience'.

Throughout my career, I have often felt confined by the conventional and tangible approach to architectural design, which primarily revolves around the physical aspects of our surroundings.

Fast-forward to today,

a master in information architecture and user experience design in my pocket and the belief that this is the way to explore a whole new world of designing spaces beyond the norm

When I'm not at the computer, I'm usually guitar playing, trekking, or hanging out with my friends with a cold one to keep us company.
